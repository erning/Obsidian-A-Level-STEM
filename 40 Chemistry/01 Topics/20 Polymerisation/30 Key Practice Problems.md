# 20 Polymerisation - Key Practice Problems

## Questions

1. Define monomer, repeat unit, and polymer in the context of addition polymerisation.

2. Write an equation for the addition polymerisation of ethene and state the repeat unit.

3. Deduce the repeat unit formed from chloroethene, $\ce{CH2=CHCl}$, and give the common abbreviation of the polymer.

4. Deduce the repeat unit formed from propene, $\ce{CH2=CHCH3}$.

5. A polymer has repeat unit $\ce{-CH2-CH(CH3)-}$. Identify the monomer.

6. A polymer has repeat unit $\ce{-CH2-C(CH3)2-}$. Identify the monomer.

7. Explain why the $\ce{C=C}$ double bond in an alkene monomer is essential for addition polymerisation.

8. State two difficulties associated with disposal of poly(alkene)s.

9. Explain why burning PVC can produce harmful combustion products.

10. A student draws the repeat unit of poly(ethene) as $\ce{-CH=CH-}$. Explain the error.
