# Group 2 - Key Practice Problems

Keep answers out of this file during active recall. Use [31 Key Practice Solutions](31%20Key%20Practice%20Solutions.md) only after attempting the questions.

## Questions

1. State the Group 2 metals included in the AS topic and the common ion they form.

2. Describe and explain the trend in reactivity of Group 2 metals from magnesium to barium.

3. Write a general equation for a Group 2 metal reacting with oxygen. Then write the equation for magnesium reacting with oxygen.

4. Write equations for magnesium with steam and calcium with cold water. Include one observation for the calcium reaction.

5. Write general equations for a Group 2 metal reacting with dilute hydrochloric acid and dilute sulfuric acid.

6. Write equations for calcium oxide reacting with water and with dilute hydrochloric acid.

7. Write equations for barium hydroxide reacting with dilute hydrochloric acid and dilute sulfuric acid.

8. Write a general equation for a Group 2 carbonate reacting with dilute hydrochloric acid. State the gas test for the gas produced.

9. Write general equations for the thermal decomposition of a Group 2 carbonate and a Group 2 nitrate.

10. State the thermal stability trend for Group 2 carbonates and nitrates from magnesium to barium.

11. State the solubility trends for Group 2 hydroxides and sulfates. Use named examples at the top and bottom of the group.

12. A white solid gives brown fumes and a gas that relights a glowing splint when heated. The solid is a Group 2 compound. Identify the compound type and write a general equation.
