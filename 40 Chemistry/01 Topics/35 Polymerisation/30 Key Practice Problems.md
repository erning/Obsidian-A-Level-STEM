# 35 Polymerisation - Key Practice Problems

1. Explain the difference between addition polymerisation and condensation polymerisation.

2. Deduce the repeat unit from $\ce{HOCH2CH2OH}$ and $\ce{HOOCCH2COOH}$.

3. Deduce the repeat unit from $\ce{HOCH2CH2COOH}$.

4. Deduce the repeat unit from $\ce{H2N(CH2)4NH2}$ and $\ce{HOOC(CH2)4COOH}$.

5. Deduce the repeat unit from $\ce{H2NCH2COOH}$.

6. A polymer section contains $\ce{-NHCH2CH2NHCOC6H4CO-}$. Identify the monomers.

7. Predict the type of polymerisation for propene, ethane-1,2-diol with ethanedioic acid, and 6-aminohexanoic acid.

8. Explain why poly(alkenes) can be difficult to biodegrade.

9. Explain why polyesters and polyamides can be biodegradable by acidic and alkaline hydrolysis.

10. State one way in which some polymers can be degraded by light.
