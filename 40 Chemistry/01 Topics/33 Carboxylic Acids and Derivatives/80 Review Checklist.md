# 33 Carboxylic Acids and Derivatives - Review Checklist

- [ ] I can state that this topic follows CAIE Chemistry 9701 Topic 33.

## Carboxylic Acids

- [ ] I can produce benzoic acid from methylbenzene using hot alkaline $\ce{KMnO4}$ followed by dilute acid.
- [ ] I can name reagents for converting carboxylic acids into acyl chlorides.
- [ ] I remember that methanoic acid and ethanedioic acid can be further oxidised.

## Acidity

- [ ] I can explain why carboxylic acids are stronger acids than phenols and alcohols.

## Acyl Chlorides

- [ ] I can write reactions of acyl chlorides with water, alcohols, phenol, ammonia and amines.
- [ ] I remember that $\ce{HCl}$ is produced in these reactions.
- [ ] I can describe the addition-elimination mechanism.
- [ ] I can compare hydrolysis of acyl chlorides, alkyl chlorides and halogenoarenes.

- [ ] I can use acyl chloride chemistry in organic synthesis and condensation polymerisation.
