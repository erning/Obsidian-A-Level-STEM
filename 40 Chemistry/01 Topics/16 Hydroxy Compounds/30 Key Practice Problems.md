# 16 Hydroxy Compounds - Key Practice Problems

1. Classify each alcohol as primary, secondary or tertiary:
   - $\ce{CH3CH2CH2OH}$
   - $\ce{CH3CH(OH)CH2CH3}$
   - $\ce{(CH3)2C(OH)CH2CH3}$

2. State reagents and conditions for making ethanol from ethene.

3. State reagents and conditions for making ethane-1,2-diol from ethene.

4. Give the product when bromoethane reacts with $\ce{NaOH(aq)}$ and heat.

5. Give the alcohol product from reducing:
   - ethanal with $\ce{NaBH4}$
   - propanone with $\ce{NaBH4}$
   - ethanoic acid with $\ce{LiAlH4}$

6. Write the equation for ethanol reacting with sodium.

7. Predict the organic product when butan-1-ol is oxidised with acidified $\ce{K2Cr2O7}$:
   - under distillation
   - under reflux

8. State the expected observation when a tertiary alcohol is warmed with acidified $\ce{K2Cr2O7}$.

9. Give the alkene product from dehydration of ethanol.

10. Name the ester made from propan-1-ol and ethanoic acid.

11. State which of ethanol, propan-1-ol and butan-2-ol give a positive tri-iodomethane test.
