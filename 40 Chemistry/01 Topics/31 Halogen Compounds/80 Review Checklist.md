- [ ] I can state that this topic follows CAIE Chemistry 9701 Topic 31.1 Halogen compounds.

## Halogenoarene Formation

- [ ] I can define halogenoarene.
- [ ] I can write equations for making chlorobenzene and bromobenzene.
- [ ] I remember the catalysts $\ce{AlCl3}$ and $\ce{AlBr3}$.

- [ ] I can compare chloroethane and chlorobenzene.
