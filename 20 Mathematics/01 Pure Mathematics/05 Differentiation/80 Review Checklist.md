# Differentiation Review Checklist

Use this checklist after the practice problems. Any unchecked item is a cue to return to the lecture notes or worked examples.

## Ideas to Recall

- [ ] Derivative as gradient function and rate of change.
- [ ] Chain rule, product rule, quotient rule, and derivatives of exponential, logarithmic, trigonometric, inverse trigonometric, and hyperbolic functions.
- [ ] Tangents, normals, stationary points, increasing and decreasing functions, and second derivatives.
- [ ] Implicit and parametric differentiation.
- [ ] Further differentiation techniques where required by 9231.

## Skills to Perform

- [ ] Differentiate functions in explicit, implicit, and parametric form.
- [ ] Solve optimisation and rates-of-change problems.
- [ ] Classify stationary points and sketch derivative information.

## Common Errors to Avoid

- [ ] Applying the chain rule incompletely.
- [ ] Classifying a stationary point from the second derivative alone when it is zero.
- [ ] Forgetting domain restrictions for logarithmic and inverse trigonometric derivatives.

## Ready to Move On When

- [ ] I can explain the purpose of Differentiation without reading the notes.
- [ ] I can choose a method before seeing a worked solution.
- [ ] I can check my answer using units, signs, domains, graph features, limiting cases, or context.
- [ ] I can connect this topic to at least one neighbouring topic.
