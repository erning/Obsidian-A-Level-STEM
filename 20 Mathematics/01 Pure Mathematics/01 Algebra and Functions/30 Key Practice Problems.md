# Algebra and Functions Key Practice Problems

These are original practice problems designed around the 9709 and 9231 algebra
and function skills. Work without looking at the solutions first.

## A. Quadratics

1. Write $3x^2-18x+20$ in completed-square form. Hence state its minimum value.

2. Find the values of $k$ for which

   $$
   x^2 + (k-2)x + k = 0
   $$

   has two distinct real roots.

3. Solve

   $$
   2x^2-5x-3 \le 0.
   $$

4. Solve

   $$
   x^4-10x^2+9=0.
   $$

5. The line $y=2x+c$ touches the curve $y=x^2-4x+1$. Find $c$.

## B. Functions

6. Let $f(x)=2x-5$ and $g(x)=x^2+1$. Find $fg(x)$ and $gf(x)$.

7. Let

   $$
   f(x)=\sqrt{3x-2}.
   $$

   State the domain of $f$.

8. Let

   $$
   f(x)=\frac{2x+1}{x-3}.
   $$

   Find $f^{-1}(x)$ and state any value excluded from the domain of $f^{-1}$.

9. Let $f(x)=x^2-4x+7$ for $x\ge 2$. Find the range of $f$ and find
   $f^{-1}(x)$.

10. The graph of $y=f(x)$ passes through $(2,5)$. State the corresponding point
    on the graph of $y=3f(x-1)-4$.

## C. Modulus and Polynomial Algebra

11. Solve

    $$
    |3x+2|=|x-6|.
    $$

12. Solve

    $$
    |x-4|\ge 6.
    $$

13. The polynomial $f(x)=x^3+ax^2-7x+6$ has factor $x-2$. Find $a$.

14. Find the remainder when

    $$
    2x^4-x^3+3x-5
    $$

    is divided by $x+2$.

15. Divide

    $$
    x^3+2x^2-5x+1
    $$

    by $x-1$, giving the quotient and remainder.

## D. Further Algebra and Rational Functions

16. The roots of

    $$
    x^3-5x^2+3x+2=0
    $$

    are $\alpha$, $\beta$, and $\gamma$. Find

    $$
    \alpha^2+\beta^2+\gamma^2.
    $$

17. The roots of $x^3-2x^2-5x+6=0$ are $\alpha$, $\beta$, and $\gamma$. Form
    the equation whose roots are $\alpha+1$, $\beta+1$, and $\gamma+1$.

18. Find the vertical asymptote and oblique asymptote of

    $$
    y=\frac{x^2-3x+4}{x-2}.
    $$

19. Decompose into partial fractions:

    $$
    \frac{7x-1}{(x+1)(2x-3)}.
    $$

20. Find the range of

    $$
    y=\frac{x}{x^2+4}.
    $$

## E. Mixed Review

21. Solve

    $$
    \frac{x+2}{x-1}>2.
    $$

22. Find the values of $m$ for which the equation

    $$
    x^2+mx+m+3=0
    $$

    has no real roots.

23. Find the inverse of

    $$
    f(x)=\frac{4-x}{2x+1}.
    $$

24. A polynomial $f(x)$ leaves remainder $4$ when divided by $x-1$ and
    remainder $-2$ when divided by $x+2$. If

    $$
    f(x)=x^3+px+q,
    $$

    find $p$ and $q$.

25. Sketch the key features of

    $$
    y=\frac{x^2+1}{x-1}.
    $$

    Include the vertical asymptote, oblique asymptote, and intercepts.
