# Trigonometry and Circular Measure Review Checklist

Use this checklist after the practice problems. Any unchecked item is a cue to return to the lecture notes or worked examples.

## Ideas to Recall

- [ ] Radian measure, arc length, and sector area.
- [ ] Trigonometric ratios, graphs, inverse trigonometric functions, and equations.
- [ ] Compound-angle, double-angle, and related identities.
- [ ] General solutions and expressions such as $a\sin\theta + b\cos\theta$.

## Skills to Perform

- [ ] Solve trigonometric equations over specified intervals.
- [ ] Use identities to simplify expressions.
- [ ] Apply arc length and sector area formulae.

## Common Errors to Avoid

- [ ] Mixing degrees and radians.
- [ ] Dropping solution families in trigonometric equations.
- [ ] Using inverse trigonometric functions without checking range.

## Ready to Move On When

- [ ] I can explain the purpose of Trigonometry and Circular Measure without reading the notes.
- [ ] I can choose a method before seeing a worked solution.
- [ ] I can check my answer using units, signs, domains, graph features, limiting cases, or context.
- [ ] I can connect this topic to at least one neighbouring topic.
