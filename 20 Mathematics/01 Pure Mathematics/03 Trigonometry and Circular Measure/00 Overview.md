# 03 Trigonometry and Circular Measure

## Core Idea

Trigonometry connects angles, periodic functions, identities, and geometry.

## Source Alignment

- 9709 1.4 Circular measure
- 9709 1.5 Trigonometry
- 9709 2.3 Trigonometry
- 9709 3.3 Trigonometry
- Coursebook route: 9709 Pure Mathematics 1 Chapters 4-5; 9709 Pure Mathematics 2 and 3 Chapter 3.

## What to Learn

- Radian measure, arc length, and sector area.
- Trigonometric ratios, graphs, inverse trigonometric functions, and equations.
- Compound-angle, double-angle, and related identities.
- General solutions and expressions such as $a\sin\theta + b\cos\theta$.

## How to Study

- Use the unit circle for signs, symmetry, and periodicity.
- Write angle units explicitly and use radians when calculus is involved.
- Verify identities by transforming one side into the other.

## Practice Directions

- Solve trigonometric equations over specified intervals.
- Use identities to simplify expressions.
- Apply arc length and sector area formulae.
