# Coordinate Geometry and Graphs Review Checklist

Use this checklist after the practice problems. Any unchecked item is a cue to return to the lecture notes or worked examples.

## Ideas to Recall

- [ ] Equations of straight lines in common forms.
- [ ] Distances, gradients, midpoints, intersections, and relationships between parallel and perpendicular lines.
- [ ] Circle equations in centre-radius and expanded forms.
- [ ] Intersections of lines, circles, and curves as solutions of equations.
- [ ] Graph transformations of $y = f(x)$.

## Skills to Perform

- [ ] Find equations of lines and circles.
- [ ] Solve line-circle and graph-intersection problems.
- [ ] Sketch transformed graphs with domain and range.

## Common Errors to Avoid

- [ ] Mixing gradient with angle.
- [ ] Forgetting tangent is perpendicular to radius at the point of contact.
- [ ] Expanding circle equations and losing the centre.

## Ready to Move On When

- [ ] I can explain the purpose of Coordinate Geometry and Graphs without reading the notes.
- [ ] I can choose a method before seeing a worked solution.
- [ ] I can check my answer using units, signs, domains, graph features, limiting cases, or context.
- [ ] I can connect this topic to at least one neighbouring topic.
