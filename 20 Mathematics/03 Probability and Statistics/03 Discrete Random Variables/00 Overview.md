# 03 Discrete Random Variables

## Core Idea

A discrete random variable assigns probabilities to countable outcomes and supports expectation and variance calculations.

## Source Alignment

- 9709 5.4 Discrete random variables
- Coursebook route: 9709 Probability and Statistics 1 chapters on discrete random variables and common discrete distributions.

## What to Learn

- Probability mass functions and cumulative probabilities.
- Expectation, variance, and standard deviation.
- Binomial and geometric distributions where included by 9709.
- Modelling assumptions behind each distribution.
- Interpreting results in context.

## How to Study

- Write the random variable definition in words before using symbols.
- Check total probability equals 1.
- Connect distribution choice to the random mechanism.

## Practice Directions

- Build probability tables.
- Calculate expectation and variance.
- Use binomial and geometric models.

## Common Traps

- Using a distribution without checking assumptions.
- Confusing $P(X=x)$ with $P(X\le x)$.
- Leaving expectation without units or context.
