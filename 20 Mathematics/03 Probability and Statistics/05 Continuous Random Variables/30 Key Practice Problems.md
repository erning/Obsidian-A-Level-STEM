# Continuous Random Variables Key Practice Problems

Work these without looking at the solutions first. Always write the support of
the density before integrating.

## A. Density Functions and Probabilities

1. A random variable $X$ has density

   $$
   f(x)=kx,\qquad 0\le x\le4.
   $$

   Find $k$ and $P(1<X<3)$.

2. A random variable $X$ has density

   $$
   f(x)=k(1+x),\qquad 0\le x\le2.
   $$

   Find $k$ and $P(X>1)$.

3. A random variable $X$ has density

   $$
   f(x)=\frac38x^2,\qquad 0\le x\le2.
   $$

   Check that this is a valid density and find $P(X<1)$.

4. A random variable $X$ has density

   $$
   f(x)=c(4-x),\qquad 0\le x\le4.
   $$

   Find $c$ and $P(1<X<3)$.

5. A random variable $X$ has density

   $$
   f(x)=
   \begin{cases}
   x, & 0\le x\le1,\\
   2-x, & 1<x\le2,\\
   0, & \text{otherwise}.
   \end{cases}
   $$

   Find $P(X<1)$ and $P(X>1.5)$.

## B. Mean, Variance, and Percentiles

6. Let

   $$
   f(x)=2x,\qquad 0\le x\le1.
   $$

   Find $E(X)$ and $\operatorname{Var}(X)$.

7. Let

   $$
   f(x)=3x^2,\qquad 0\le x\le1.
   $$

   Find $E(X)$ and $\operatorname{Var}(X)$.

8. Let

   $$
   f(x)=\frac12,\qquad 0\le x\le2.
   $$

   Find the median and the $75$th percentile.

9. Let

   $$
   f(x)=\frac38x^2,\qquad 0\le x\le2.
   $$

   Find the median.

10. Let

    $$
    f(x)=\frac14(1+x),\qquad 0\le x\le2.
    $$

    Find $E(X)$ and $\operatorname{Var}(X)$.

## C. CDFs and Piecewise Densities

11. A random variable $X$ has CDF

    $$
    F(x)=
    \begin{cases}
    0, & x<0,\\
    \frac{x^2}{4}, & 0\le x\le2,\\
    1, & x>2.
    \end{cases}
    $$

    Find the PDF and $P(0.5<X<1.5)$.

12. A random variable $X$ has density

    $$
    f(x)=
    \begin{cases}
    \frac13, & 0\le x\le1,\\
    \frac23, & 1<x\le2,\\
    0, & \text{otherwise}.
    \end{cases}
    $$

    Find the CDF and $P(0.5<X<1.5)$.

13. A random variable $X$ has density

    $$
    f(x)=
    \begin{cases}
    kx, & 0\le x\le2,\\
    k(4-x), & 2<x\le4,\\
    0, & \text{otherwise}.
    \end{cases}
    $$

    Find $k$ and $P(1<X<3)$.

14. A random variable $X$ has CDF $F(x)=x^3$ for $0\le x\le1$, with
    $F(x)=0$ for $x<0$ and $F(x)=1$ for $x>1$. Find the PDF and the median.

15. A random variable $X$ has density

    $$
    f(x)=\frac{1}{x^2},\qquad x\ge1.
    $$

    Find $P(X>4)$ and $P(1<X<3)$.

## D. Functions and Related Variables

16. Let $X$ have density $f(x)=2x$ for $0\le x\le1$. Find $E(1+3X)$.

17. Let $X$ have density $f(x)=2x$ for $0\le x\le1$, and let $Y=X^2$.
    Find the CDF and PDF of $Y$.

18. Let $X$ have CDF $F_X(x)=x^2$ for $0\le x\le1$, with $F_X(x)=0$ for
    $x<0$ and $F_X(x)=1$ for $x>1$. Let $Y=X^3$. Find the CDF and PDF of
    $Y$.

19. Let $X$ have density $f(x)=3x^2$ for $0\le x\le1$, and let $Y=1-X$.
    Find the CDF and PDF of $Y$.

20. Let $X$ have density $f(x)=2x$ for $0\le x\le1$. Find $P(X^2<0.25)$
    and $E(X^2)$.
