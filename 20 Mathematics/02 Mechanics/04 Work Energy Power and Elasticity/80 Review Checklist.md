# Work, Energy, Power and Elasticity Review Checklist

Use this after the worked examples and practice problems. Revisit [[20 Mathematics/02 Mechanics/04 Work Energy Power and Elasticity/10 Lecture Notes|Work, Energy, Power and Elasticity Lecture Notes]] if any item is uncertain.

## Ideas to Recall

- [ ] Work done by a constant force is $Fd\cos\theta$.
- [ ] Kinetic energy is $\frac12mv^2$.
- [ ] Gravitational potential energy changes by $mg\Delta h$.
- [ ] Mechanical energy is conserved only when no non-conservative work is done.
- [ ] Friction and resistance do negative work on the moving body.
- [ ] Power is rate of doing work, and $P=Fv$ uses the force component in the direction of motion.
- [ ] Hooke's law for a string or spring is $T=\frac{\lambda x}{l}$.
- [ ] Elastic potential energy is $\frac{\lambda x^2}{2l}$.

## Skills to Perform

- [ ] Decide whether a force does positive, negative, or zero work.
- [ ] Write a complete energy equation before substituting numbers.
- [ ] Separate kinetic energy, gravitational potential energy, elastic energy, and external work.
- [ ] Use work done against friction correctly as an energy loss.
- [ ] Use average power and instantaneous $P=Fv$ in the right contexts.
- [ ] Find engine force from power at a given speed.
- [ ] Compute extension from current length minus natural length.
- [ ] Use elastic energy to find speed or maximum extension.

## Common Errors to Avoid

- [ ] Using energy conservation when friction or resistance does work.
- [ ] Using the current length instead of extension in Hooke's law.
- [ ] Forgetting the factor $\frac12$ in kinetic or elastic energy.
- [ ] Mixing newtons and joules in the same equation.
- [ ] Applying $P=Fv$ when the force is not in the direction of velocity.
- [ ] Treating equilibrium extension and maximum dynamic extension as the same thing.

## Ready to Move On When

- [ ] I can choose a start state and end state for an energy method.
- [ ] I can account for every joule of work, kinetic energy, gravitational potential energy, and elastic potential energy.
- [ ] I can solve power questions using either work over time or $P=Fv$.
- [ ] I can solve elastic string or spring questions without confusing length and extension.
- [ ] I can check a speed, power, or extension for physical plausibility.
