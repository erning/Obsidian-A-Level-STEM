# Integrated Sequence

9709 and 9231 can be studied together, but not as a simple copy of examination order. A better route follows conceptual dependency: learn a core tool, then attach the further extension when the foundation is ready.

## Stage 1: Algebraic and Graphical Language

1. [[20 Mathematics/01 Pure Mathematics/01 Algebra and Functions/00 Overview|Algebra and Functions]]
2. [[20 Mathematics/01 Pure Mathematics/02 Coordinate Geometry and Graphs/00 Overview|Coordinate Geometry and Graphs]]
3. [[20 Mathematics/01 Pure Mathematics/03 Trigonometry and Circular Measure/00 Overview|Trigonometry and Circular Measure]]
4. [[20 Mathematics/01 Pure Mathematics/04 Sequences Series and Binomial Expansions/00 Overview|Sequences, Series and Binomial Expansions]]

Goal: make symbols, functions, graphs, and elementary structure feel natural.

## Stage 2: Calculus and Physical Meaning

1. [[20 Mathematics/01 Pure Mathematics/05 Differentiation/00 Overview|Differentiation]]
2. [[20 Mathematics/01 Pure Mathematics/06 Integration/00 Overview|Integration]]
3. [[20 Mathematics/01 Pure Mathematics/07 Logarithms Exponentials and Numerical Methods/00 Overview|Logarithms, Exponentials and Numerical Methods]]
4. [[20 Mathematics/02 Mechanics/01 Forces and Equilibrium/00 Overview|Forces and Equilibrium]]
5. [[20 Mathematics/02 Mechanics/02 Kinematics and Newtonian Motion/00 Overview|Kinematics and Newtonian Motion]]
6. [[20 Mathematics/02 Mechanics/04 Work Energy Power and Elasticity/00 Overview|Work, Energy, Power and Elasticity]]

Goal: connect rate of change, accumulated change, force, motion, energy, and modelling.

## Stage 3: Space, Linear Structure and Complex Numbers

1. [[20 Mathematics/01 Pure Mathematics/08 Vectors/00 Overview|Vectors]]
2. [[20 Mathematics/01 Pure Mathematics/10 Matrices and Transformations/00 Overview|Matrices and Transformations]]
3. [[20 Mathematics/01 Pure Mathematics/09 Complex Numbers/00 Overview|Complex Numbers]]
4. [[20 Mathematics/01 Pure Mathematics/11 Proof and Mathematical Induction/00 Overview|Proof and Mathematical Induction]]
5. [[20 Mathematics/01 Pure Mathematics/12 Polar Coordinates and Parametric Curves/00 Overview|Polar Coordinates and Parametric Curves]]

Goal: move from computation to structure: spaces, transformations, proof, and alternative coordinate systems.

## Stage 4: Probability and Statistics

1. [[20 Mathematics/03 Probability and Statistics/01 Data Representation and Summary/00 Overview|Data Representation and Summary]]
2. [[20 Mathematics/03 Probability and Statistics/02 Counting and Probability/00 Overview|Counting and Probability]]
3. [[20 Mathematics/03 Probability and Statistics/03 Discrete Random Variables/00 Overview|Discrete Random Variables]]
4. [[20 Mathematics/03 Probability and Statistics/04 Normal and Poisson Distributions/00 Overview|Normal and Poisson Distributions]]
5. [[20 Mathematics/03 Probability and Statistics/05 Continuous Random Variables/00 Overview|Continuous Random Variables]]
6. [[20 Mathematics/03 Probability and Statistics/06 Sampling Estimation and Hypothesis Tests/00 Overview|Sampling, Estimation and Hypothesis Tests]]
7. [[20 Mathematics/03 Probability and Statistics/07 Chi Squared Nonparametric and PGF/00 Overview|Chi-Squared, Non-Parametric Tests and PGF]]

Goal: understand distributions as models that generate data, then use samples to reason under uncertainty.

## Stage 5: Further Modelling

1. [[20 Mathematics/01 Pure Mathematics/13 Further Calculus and Differential Equations/00 Overview|Further Calculus and Differential Equations]]
2. [[20 Mathematics/02 Mechanics/03 Momentum Collisions and Impulse/00 Overview|Momentum, Collisions and Impulse]]
3. [[20 Mathematics/02 Mechanics/05 Projectiles and Circular Motion/00 Overview|Projectiles and Circular Motion]]
4. [[20 Mathematics/02 Mechanics/06 Rigid Bodies and Variable Forces/00 Overview|Rigid Bodies and Variable Forces]]

Goal: combine calculus, geometry, mechanics, and modelling in more flexible systems.
