# Integrated Sequence

9709 and 9231 can be studied together as one connected sequence. The route follows conceptual dependency: learn a core tool, then attach the further extension when the foundation is ready.

## Stage 1: Algebraic and Graphical Language

1. [Algebra and Functions](../01%20Pure%20Mathematics/01%20Algebra%20and%20Functions/00%20Overview.md)
2. [Coordinate Geometry and Graphs](../01%20Pure%20Mathematics/02%20Coordinate%20Geometry%20and%20Graphs/00%20Overview.md)
3. [Trigonometry and Circular Measure](../01%20Pure%20Mathematics/03%20Trigonometry%20and%20Circular%20Measure/00%20Overview.md)
4. [Sequences, Series and Binomial Expansions](../01%20Pure%20Mathematics/04%20Sequences%20Series%20and%20Binomial%20Expansions/00%20Overview.md)

Goal: make symbols, functions, graphs, and elementary structure feel natural.

## Stage 2: Calculus and Physical Meaning

1. [Differentiation](../01%20Pure%20Mathematics/05%20Differentiation/00%20Overview.md)
2. [Integration](../01%20Pure%20Mathematics/06%20Integration/00%20Overview.md)
3. [Logarithms, Exponentials and Numerical Methods](../01%20Pure%20Mathematics/07%20Logarithms%20Exponentials%20and%20Numerical%20Methods/00%20Overview.md)
4. [Forces and Equilibrium](../02%20Mechanics/01%20Forces%20and%20Equilibrium/00%20Overview.md)
5. [Kinematics and Newtonian Motion](../02%20Mechanics/02%20Kinematics%20and%20Newtonian%20Motion/00%20Overview.md)
6. [Work, Energy, Power and Elasticity](../02%20Mechanics/04%20Work%20Energy%20Power%20and%20Elasticity/00%20Overview.md)

Goal: connect rate of change, accumulated change, force, motion, energy, and modelling.

## Stage 3: Space, Linear Structure and Complex Numbers

1. [Vectors](../01%20Pure%20Mathematics/08%20Vectors/00%20Overview.md)
2. [Matrices and Transformations](../01%20Pure%20Mathematics/10%20Matrices%20and%20Transformations/00%20Overview.md)
3. [Complex Numbers](../01%20Pure%20Mathematics/09%20Complex%20Numbers/00%20Overview.md)
4. [Proof and Mathematical Induction](../01%20Pure%20Mathematics/11%20Proof%20and%20Mathematical%20Induction/00%20Overview.md)
5. [Polar Coordinates and Parametric Curves](../01%20Pure%20Mathematics/12%20Polar%20Coordinates%20and%20Parametric%20Curves/00%20Overview.md)

Goal: move from computation to structure: spaces, transformations, proof, and alternative coordinate systems.

## Stage 4: Probability and Statistics

1. [Data Representation and Summary](../03%20Probability%20and%20Statistics/01%20Data%20Representation%20and%20Summary/00%20Overview.md)
2. [Counting and Probability](../03%20Probability%20and%20Statistics/02%20Counting%20and%20Probability/00%20Overview.md)
3. [Discrete Random Variables](../03%20Probability%20and%20Statistics/03%20Discrete%20Random%20Variables/00%20Overview.md)
4. [Normal and Poisson Distributions](../03%20Probability%20and%20Statistics/04%20Normal%20and%20Poisson%20Distributions/00%20Overview.md)
5. [Continuous Random Variables](../03%20Probability%20and%20Statistics/05%20Continuous%20Random%20Variables/00%20Overview.md)
6. [Sampling, Estimation and Hypothesis Tests](../03%20Probability%20and%20Statistics/06%20Sampling%20Estimation%20and%20Hypothesis%20Tests/00%20Overview.md)
7. [Chi-Squared, Non-Parametric Tests and PGF](../03%20Probability%20and%20Statistics/07%20Chi%20Squared%20Nonparametric%20and%20PGF/00%20Overview.md)

Goal: understand distributions as models that generate data, then use samples to reason under uncertainty.

## Stage 5: Further Modelling

1. [Further Calculus and Differential Equations](../01%20Pure%20Mathematics/13%20Further%20Calculus%20and%20Differential%20Equations/00%20Overview.md)
2. [Momentum, Collisions and Impulse](../02%20Mechanics/03%20Momentum%20Collisions%20and%20Impulse/00%20Overview.md)
3. [Projectiles and Circular Motion](../02%20Mechanics/05%20Projectiles%20and%20Circular%20Motion/00%20Overview.md)
4. [Rigid Bodies and Variable Forces](../02%20Mechanics/06%20Rigid%20Bodies%20and%20Variable%20Forces/00%20Overview.md)

Goal: combine calculus, geometry, mechanics, and modelling in more flexible systems.

- Link one mathematics topic to one physics topic each week.
- Move repeated errors into [Problem Solving Patterns](../04%20Reference/Problem%20Solving%20Patterns.md).
