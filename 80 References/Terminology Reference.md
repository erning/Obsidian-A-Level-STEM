# Terminology Reference

This note is a bilingual lookup reference. Subject notes in `10 Physics/` and
`20 Mathematics/` should use the standard CAIE English term first. Chinese names
are kept here for comparison with mainland China usage, not as the default
writing style for the main notes.

When adding or changing an English term, check the relevant CAIE syllabus or
coursebook in `assets/` first. Do not infer an English term by translating a
Chinese phrase.
