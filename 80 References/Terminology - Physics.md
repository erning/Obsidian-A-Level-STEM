# Terminology - Physics

This note is a bilingual lookup reference for CAIE Physics 9702. Subject notes under `10 Physics/` should use the standard CAIE English term first. Chinese names are kept here for comparison with mainland China usage, not as the default writing style for the main notes.

When adding or changing an English term, check the relevant CAIE syllabus or coursebook in `assets/` first. Do not infer an English term by translating a Chinese phrase.
