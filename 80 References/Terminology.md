# Terminology

This note is the index for bilingual terminology lookup notes. Subject notes should use the standard CAIE English term first. Chinese names are kept in the linked terminology notes for comparison with mainland China usage, not as the default writing style for the main notes.

When adding or changing an English term, check the relevant CAIE syllabus or coursebook in `assets/` first. Do not infer an English term by translating a Chinese phrase.
